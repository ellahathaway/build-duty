# Issue Writing

Produce issue metadata fields for new or updated work items.

## Required fields
- `summary`: concise statement of cause and impact
- `issueSignature`: short stable causal signature
- `correlationRationale`: explicit why grouped signals share one cause
- `resolutionCriteria`: concrete condition to mark resolved

## Quality rules
- Focus on cause, not symptom list.
- Keep wording specific and actionable.
- Avoid vague labels like component names alone.
